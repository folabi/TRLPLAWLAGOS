Litigation

TRLPLAW prides itself as one of the top-notch firms in Nigeria that have a robust and seasoned Dispute Resolution Team. We provide specialized legal services to high net worth individuals and diverse corporate bodies.

In Litigation, the firm has represented clients in high profile political and commercial cases at the High court, Court of Appeal and apex court. The firm has also been engaged in rendering pro bono services. Members of the team have a diligent, pragmatic and solution driven approach to litigation. TRLPLAW has enjoyed high success rates in very intricate and complex commercial disputes.

In commercially sensitive disputes, the firm encourages an alternative means of dispute resolution. Hence, the firm has a specialist team with experience in Mediation and Arbitration. We have a wide variety of innovative dispute resolution models. The Team is made up of well-seasoned negotiators and mediators with local and international experience. We have the ability to interact with people from different ethnic, cultural and professional backgrounds, and we benefit from our global interactions and networking.

Our understanding of cultural differences enable us to work well with local counsel, when moving a case through the international mediation and arbitration process.
